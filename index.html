MLNLPWeb App
The Student Dropout Prevention Web Application is designed to identify and prevent student dropouts using Machine Learning and NLP techniques. It collects data such as attendance, marks, fee status, and student feedback to classify students as Risk, Danger, or Non-Risk. The system sends alerts to mentors for early intervention and counselling, and also provides academic, emotional, and financial support to ensure student success and retention.
